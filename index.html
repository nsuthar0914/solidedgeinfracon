Corporate Introduction

Professional construction solutions for infrastructure growth.

SolidEdge Infracon LLP is a professionally managed infrastructure and construction company committed to delivering quality, reliability, and timely execution across civil engineering and infrastructure projects.

The LLP has been established with the vision of providing professional and technically sound construction solutions in the fields of infrastructure development, water supply systems, industrial works, commercial buildings, and public utility projects.

The organization is backed by experienced professionals having practical exposure in execution, coordination, supervision, and management of construction and infrastructure works. With a focus on quality workmanship, efficient resource management, and client satisfaction, SolidEdge Infracon LLP aims to establish itself as a trusted execution partner in both government and private sector projects.

Areas of Expertise

Focused infrastructure capabilities across civil construction, water supply, irrigation, sewerage, RCC structures, EPC execution, E&M works, and operation and maintenance.

01

Infrastructure Development

Execution of comprehensive infrastructure projects including roads, utilities, urban development, industrial infrastructure, and public utility works with a focus on quality, timely delivery, and sustainable construction practices.

02

Water Supply & Irrigation Projects

Expertise in execution of water supply schemes, pumping stations, transmission pipelines, distribution networks, overhead reservoirs, intake wells, WTPs, canal systems, lift irrigation, and allied hydraulic infrastructure.

03

Sewerage & Drainage Systems

Construction and development of sewerage networks, STPs, storm water drainage systems, underground drainage works, effluent management systems, and urban sanitation infrastructure.

04

RCC Structures

Specialized capabilities in design coordination and execution of reinforced cement concrete structures including reservoirs, treatment plants, industrial foundations, retaining walls, buildings, elevated structures, and heavy civil works.

05

EPC, Turnkey Execution & Project Management

End-to-end project execution covering engineering, procurement, construction, testing, commissioning, planning, coordination, quality assurance, billing, contract management, and timely project delivery on EPC and turnkey basis.

06

Industrial & Commercial Construction

Development of industrial facilities, warehouses, commercial buildings, institutional structures, factory infrastructure, utility buildings, and associated civil and structural works.

07

E & M (Electro-Mechanical Works)

Installation, testing, commissioning, and maintenance of electro-mechanical systems including pumps, motors, panels, transformers, instrumentation, SCADA integration, and associated electrical infrastructure.

08

Operation & Maintenance (Water, Sewerage & Irrigation Systems)

Comprehensive O&M services for water supply systems, sewage treatment facilities, pumping stations, irrigation schemes, pipelines, and electro-mechanical installations ensuring efficient and uninterrupted operations.
